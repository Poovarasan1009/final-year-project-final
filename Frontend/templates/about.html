About Our Project

Revolutionizing Academic Assessment with AI

Project Overview

The Intelligent Descriptive Answer Evaluation System is a BE Final Year Project that leverages cutting-edge Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning techniques to automate the assessment of descriptive answers. Our system mimics human grading with unprecedented accuracy while providing detailed feedback to help students improve.

Unlike traditional automated systems that rely on keyword matching, our 4-layer evaluation approach analyzes conceptual understanding, semantic similarity, structural coherence, and answer completeness.

Key Features

4-Layer Evaluation

Conceptual, Semantic, Structural, and Completeness analysis for comprehensive assessment.

AI-Powered Analysis

Advanced NLP models including BERT, Sentence Transformers, and custom domain adaptation.

Multi-User Platform

Separate portals for Admin, Teachers, and Students with role-based access control.
